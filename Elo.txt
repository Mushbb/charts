1) Every new player is start with 2000 rating.
2) Change of Elo rating is depends on difference between players and K-value.
3) K-value is modified by a player's current rating.
(top players' rating is hardly change, and also new players' rating is easily change.)

Elo < 2050  : K = 20
Elo < 2100  : K = 18
Elo < 2150  : K = 16
Elo < 2200  : K = 12
Elo < 2250  : K = 11
Elo >= 2250 : K = 10
